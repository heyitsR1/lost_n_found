About King's Lost N Found

Helping students find their lost belongings and return found items to their rightful owners.

Our Mission

At King's Lost N Found, we believe that every lost item has a story and every found item has an owner waiting to be reunited with it. Our platform serves as a bridge between those who have lost something valuable and those who have found items that don't belong to them.

We're committed to creating a safe, reliable, and user-friendly environment where students can quickly report lost items, search for their belongings, and help others by reporting found items.

How It Works

1
Report Lost Items

Create a detailed post with photos and description of your lost item.

2
Report Found Items

Help return found items to their owners by posting them on our platform.

3
Search & Connect

Browse through lost and found items to find matches for your situation.

4
Reunite & Celebrate

Connect with the owner or finder to arrange the return of items.

Platform Features

Advanced Search

Search by category, location, item type, and keywords to find exactly what you're looking for.

Photo Support

Upload multiple photos of your items to help with identification and verification.

Urgent Alerts

Mark items as urgent to get priority visibility and faster responses.

Secure Contact

Safe messaging system to connect with item owners or finders without sharing personal details.

Mobile Friendly

Access the platform from any device with our responsive design.

Real-time Updates

Get instant notifications and updates on your posted items.

Safety Guidelines

Important Safety Information
  • Always meet in public, well-lit areas when arranging item exchanges
  • Verify the identity of the person you're meeting
  • Don't share personal information until you're sure about the other party
  • Consider meeting on campus or in campus security presence
  • Trust your instincts - if something feels wrong, don't proceed
